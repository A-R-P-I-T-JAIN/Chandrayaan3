Chandrayaan-3

Timeline

July 6

ISRO announces the launch date of Mission Chandrayaan-3 as July 14 from Sriharikota's second pad.

July 14

Chandrayaan-3 was launched from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre in Sriharikota on July 14, on board the LVM-3 rocket.

August 5

Chandrayaan 3 sends first image of moon.

August 17

The Landing Module successfully detached from the Propulsion Module (PM)

August 19

Chandrayaan-3 inches closer to the moon

August 23

India's Chandrayaan-3 finally lands on the moon.
